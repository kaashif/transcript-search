    There is no advanced search at the moment. The data is just stored in an SQL database, so let me just tell you what the columns are:

    Field Description
    series Possible values: "atl", "sg1", "tos", "tng", "ds9", "voy", "ent"
    season_number Season number
    episode_number Episode number
    episode_title Episode title
    scene_number A "scene", as the parser sees it, is a list of consecutive lines that all happen in the same place. An episode is a list of scenes.
    line_number This is the line number in the scene.
    person Who said this line (or "ANNOTATION", meaning a stage direction or something else)
    speech The line of speech itself (or annotation)
    present Space delimited list of who is present when the line is spoken.
    place Where the scene takes place
